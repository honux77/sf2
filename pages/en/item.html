Items

Important Items


Wooden Panel

Location: Take the raft to the eastern desert and bring the old man with you. After the battle in Ribble, visit the shrine; it's in a chest after the old man leaves.
Use: Use it on the large tree in Ribble to reveal a hidden entrance. Use the A-button menu (Item -> Use) to interact. (C-button will not work). Inside, you will find the Achilles Sword.


Achilles Sword

Location: In a chest behind the entrance revealed by the Wooden Panel.
Use: Equipping it on Bowie makes him a formidable swordsman. Show it to Rohde in Hassan to have him join. This sword is the only weapon capable of harming Taros in the Ancient Shrine.


Dry Stone

Location: Near the furnace in the Dwarven Cave.
Use: Used to enter the Dwarven Village on Grans Island. After the Red Baron battle, find a shallow stream to the north and use the stone to dry up the water. Use the A-button menu (Item -> Use).


Cotton Balloon

Location: Appears in a chest after winning the Chessboard battle.
Use: Go to the tip of the fountain pen in Creed's Mansion and use it to escape.


Cannon

Location: Bring a Fairy to the Dwarven Cave and talk to the dwarf near the sick one.
Use: Combined with Dynamite, it is used to blast open the cave blocked by rocks north of Groundseal.


Dynamite

Location: After leaving Creed's, return to Granseal and talk to Astral. Then speak to the inventor standing near the weapon shop.
Use: Combined with the Cannon to blast open the blocked cave entrance.


Arm of Golem

Location: Found in the northwest corner of Moun, near the left entrance. Search (A-button -> Search) to find it.
Use: Give it to the Golem in the Magic Cave near Roft to have him join your party.


Sky Orb

Location: Defeat Cameela in Nazca.
Use: Required to operate the Nazca Ship. Enter the ship through the "eye" of the giant bird-shaped drawing and use the orb at the cockpit on the far left.


Force Sword / Chaos Breaker

Location: In the Ancient Shrine south of Groundseal (old Granseal). Enter through the same path Slade took in the prologue and Search the pedestal.
Use: Used at the Devil Statue to enter the inner sanctum of Groundseal.


Promotion Items


Warrior Pride

Location: Behind the eagle emblem in the Granseal strategy room.
Target: Jaha, Randolf.
Benefit: Promote to Baron. Increases Movement by 1 and allows use of swords.


Pegasus Wing

Location: In the Pacalon Castle, follow the carpet down and search the area to the right.
Target: Chester, Rick, Eric.
Benefit: Promote to Pegasus Knight. Grants the ability to fly.


Vigor Ball

Location 1: Chest in the northwest of the Fairy Woods.
Location 2: Search the lone tree where you found Sheela.
Target: Sarah, Karna.
Benefit: Promote to Master Monk. Allows use of powerful gloves and high combat stats.


Secret Book

Location: Second floor of the Fairy Woods house, on the right bookshelf.
Target: Kazin, Tyrin.
Benefit: Promote to Sorcerer. Grants powerful single-target summon magic.


Silver Tank

Location: Chest in the dark cave where you fought Hobgoblins on the way to Bedoe.
Target: Elric, Janet.
Benefit: Promote to Brass Gunner. High defense but lower agility.


Stat-Boosting Items (Permanent Effects)


Power Water: Increases Attack (ATT) by 2-4.

Protect Milk: Increases Defense (DEF) by 2-4.

Quick Chicken: Increases Agility (AGI) by 2-4.

Bright Honey: Increases MP by 2-4.

Cheerful Bread: Increases HP by 2-4.

Running Pimiento: Increases Movement (MOV) by 2. (Note: Use after promotion).

Brave Apple: Increases Level by 1.


Battle-Only & Recovery Items

Angel Wing: Retreat from battle (same as Egress).

Shining Ball: ~20 damage to one enemy.

Blizzard: ~33 damage to one enemy.

Holy Thunder: ~50 damage to one enemy.

Medical Herb: Restores 10 HP.
Medical Seed: Restores 20 HP.
Medical Drop: Restores 30 HP.

Healing Water: Fully restores one ally's HP.

Healing Rain: Fully restores all allies' HP.
Antidote: Cures poison.
Fairy Powder: Cures all status ailments.

Fairy Tear: Restores 20 MP.

Light of Hope: Fully restores all allies' MP. (JP Version only).


Rings & Accessories


Chirrup Sandals: Squeaks while walking. May draw enemy fire.

Power Ring: ATT +5. Use to cast Attack.

Quick Ring: AGI +5. Use to cast Boost 1.

Protect Ring: DEF +5. Use to cast Boost 1.

Running Ring: MOV +2.

White Ring: DEF +10. Use to cast Aura 2. (Bowie and Vicars only).

Black Ring: ATT +10, Cursed. Use to cast Blaze 2.

Evil Ring: ATT +15, Cursed. Use to cast Bolt 2.

Life Ring: Restores 5 HP per turn. (JP Version only).
