Frequently Asked Questions

Question: After leaving the bird kingdom (Bedoe), a bird-like creature (Peter) is following me, but I don't know where to go.
Answer: You need to talk to the village elder. Talk to him two or three times, not just once.


Question: I took the blind man (Krazit) on the boat and killed the Kraken. What now?
Answer: Go to the Ancient Ruins in the eastern desert and talk to the old man (Kazim). Make him follow you.
Then go to the ruins located just below the village where you got the archer May. He will open it for you.
After the old man leaves, obtain the Wooden Panel.

Next, go to the village above and stand in front of the large tree. Use the Wooden Panel on the tree to open the entrance.
You must open the menu with the A button, select Item, choose Use, and then select the Wooden Panel. C button will not work.



Inside, obtain the Achilles Sword and equip it to Bowie.

Then go to the village slightly below where you fought the Kraken. Talk to Rohde on the second floor in the south of the village. He will recognize the sword and join your party.

Now you can go to the Taros Shrine in the west and fight Taros.
Only Bowie equipped with the Achilles Sword can defeat Taros.

Question: I reached the place with the giant (Taros), but I can't progress.
Answer: See the answer to the question above.

Question: I got the Caravan. Where to go next?
Answer: From the temple where you got the Caravan, follow the mountain path upwards to a gravelly riverbank. Go up through there.

Question: Where is the Fairy Woods?
Answer: Go to the left of the gravelly area while in the Caravan. A dense forest will appear. Look for a slightly faint tile and enter it.

Question: How do I get to the Fairy Woods bonus stage?
Answer: In the Fairy Woods, follow the hidden tree path clockwise. From where the last treasure chest is, go up slightly and then to the right.




Question: I got the archer Elric, but I can't get to Creed's Mansion.
Answer: Go straight up from the pond where you rescued Elric. He will open the path for you. Enter the cave to head towards Creed's Mansion.

Question: After leaving Creed's Mansion and returning to Granseal, how do I break the rocks to the north?
Answer: First, make the spirit follow you, then go to the Dwarf Cave and heal the sick dwarf. He will give you the Cannon.
Then go to Granseal Castle and make Astral follow you.
Next, talk to the person to the left of the weapon shop in Granseal, and they will give you Dynamite.
With both items, you can blast through the rocks to the north.

Question: How do I find the Golem's Arm?
Answer: Search the northwesternmost part of Moun village. Press the A button and use the SEARCH command.


Question: How do I operate the Nazca Ship?
Answer: After obtaining the Sky Orb, enter the eye of the bird-shaped structure. Go to the far left and use the Sky Orb (A button -> ITEM -> USE).


Question: I want to forge Mithril weapons. How do I find the Dwarf Village?
Answer: After the battle with Red Baron, go to the riverbank to the north. Look for a flat section of the river and use the Dry Stone to dry up the water and enter the Dwarf Village.
You must use the A button menu: ITEM -> USE -> Dry Stone. C button will not work.


Question: How do I get the Force Sword?
Answer: Go to the Ancient Temple south of Galam/Old Granseal. Enter through where Slade went in the prologue to reach the Holy Sword.
Approach it, press the A button, and select SEARCH.

Question: Where is the Devil's Head that requires the Holy Sword?
Answer: Exit Galam Castle to the south, go right, then down. You will see it there.

Question: How do I use the Force Sword on the Devil's Head?
Answer: Stand in front of it, press the A button, and select SEARCH.

Question: When is the best time to promote characters? How do I use promotion items?
Answer: Promoting at level 40 gives the best stats, but it's very hard to reach.
Promoting at 40 can also make the game too easy. For a balanced experience, we recommend promoting around level 20.
To use special promotion items, just have the character carry it, and the priest will ask if you want to use it during promotion.

Question: Why can't Lemon equip any weapons?
Answer: This is a bug in the English ROM/emulation. (Editor use is not covered here.)

Question: There is a treasure chest surrounded by water in Mitula's Shrine. How do I get it? Same for the Prism Flower battle...
Answer: Both are only obtainable in the Japanese version.

Question: How do I get the Light of Hope and Life Ring?
Answer: They are only available in the Japanese version.

Question: Why can't I use the Taros Sword or Camilla's Iron Ball?
Answer: Because they are exclusive to those bosses.

Question: Please recommend some good characters.
Answer: All characters can be good if raised well, but personally...
Bowie, Karna, Sarah, Sheela, Slade, Gerhalt, May, one of the Pegasus Knights, Baron, Randolf, Tyrin, Peter, Rohde.
