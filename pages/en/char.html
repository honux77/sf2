Characters


*The numbers in parentheses next to spells indicate the level required to learn that spell. These are calculated by adding pre-promotion and post-promotion levels. For characters who join already promoted, subtract 20 from their indicated level. For Slade and Summoners, levels refer to their post-promotion level.

Bowie


Name: Bowie
Appearances: Shining Force II, Shining Wisdom
Pre-Promotion: SWMN (Swordsman)
Post-Promotion: HERO (Hero)
Equipment: Swords
Joins: From the start
Spells: Egress (1), Bolt (22 31 42 51)
Description: The protagonist of Shining Force II. Though he appears younger than other heroes, his skill is unmatched. His attack power grows rapidly with training. Bolt (Spark) becomes a powerful asset later on, though his MP pool is limited. He can equip all swords and the White Ring, giving him high defense. If Bowie falls in battle, the party retreats, forcing a restart of the battle.
Rating: 9/10

Sarah


"Listen, you're our leader. If you lose, we all lose, okay?"
"Did you notice how short Jaha is?"
Name: Sarah
Appearances: Shining Force II, Shining Wisdom
Pre-Promotion: PRST (Priest)
Post-Promotion: VICR (Vicar) or MMNK (Master Monk)
Equipment: Staff or Gloves (as Master Monk)
Joins: At the School
Spells: Heal (1 7 22 40), Detox (4 13 33 45), Blast (10 16 25 36), Slow (19 29)
Description: Joins early alongside Chester. She is the primary healer for the first half of the game. Using Heal regularly, even when not strictly necessary, helps her gain experience quickly. Promoting her to Master Monk turns her into a powerhouse, combining combat prowess with recovery spells. While Karna's Aura or Sheila's Boost might be more flashy, Sarah is a reliable combat medic.
Rating: 9/10

Chester


"(Neigh)... I really love being in battle!"
"I'm much taller than Jaha. Hehe... (Neigh)"
Name: Chester
Appearances: Shining Force II
Pre-Promotion: KNT (Knight)
Post-Promotion: PLDN (Paladin) or PGNT (Pegasus Knight)
Equipment: Spears or Lances

Description: Your first Centaur unit. He has excellent mobility and balanced stats. Using the Pegasus Wing to promote him to Pegasus Knight grants him flight, making him incredibly versatile. Major Bosses

Kraken

Battle: On the raft heading to Hassan.
Special Attack: Bubble Breath (3-tile range)
Strategy: A notorious early-game difficulty spike. Eliminate the tentacles with front-line units and ranged support while Peter and Luke move quickly to strike the head. Keep Kiwi away, as one bubble will defeat him.

Taros


Battle: Ancient Shrine for the Caravan.
Special Attack: Bolt Level 2 via Taros Sword.
Strategy: Only Bowie with the Achilles Sword can damage him. High HP and healing items are essential.

Zeon


Battle: The Final Battle.
Special Attack: Demon Breath Level 2.
Strategy: He is stationary, but Demon Breath is devastating. Keep your units spread out to avoid multi-target damage. Strike hard when he's low.

Other Characters

King Granseal

The wise King of Granseal who becomes distraught after his daughter's kidnapping.

Astral

Your advisor and mentor throughout the journey.

Elis

The Princess of Granseal whose kidnapping kicks off the main plot.

Hawel

Kazin's mentor and a great archaeologist. His research leads to the game's central conflict.

Volcannon

The God of Life and Creator of Parmecia. Though gruff, he cares deeply for his creation.

Creed

A former demon general who now helps those in need. His mansion is home to several potential allies.

Mitula

The Goddess of Nature who governs the weather and assists the heroes.
