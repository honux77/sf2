Mithril



During your journey, you will find small, grey stones. These are Mithril pieces.
There are a total of 15 Mithril pieces in the game.
These can be used in the late game to craft powerful weapons for your party members.
While cursed weapons might have higher attack power, they often drain health or cause paralysis, making Mithril weapons the most reliable elite equipment in the game.

How to Use Mithril

First, you need the Dry Stone, found near the furnace in the Dwarven Cave near the Fairy Woods.




Late in the game, after defeating the Red Baron, find a shallow stream to the north. Use the Dry Stone (A-button -> Item -> Use) to reveal the secret entrance to the Dwarven Village.


Inside the village, find the blacksmith's workshop located up the stairs in the back.


Talk to the blacksmith to begin crafting.

Mithril Locations

1. **Ancient Tower**: Search behind the tower on the west side of Granseal (before the earthquake).
2. **Ancient Shrine**: On a mountain peak near the shrine south of Granseal.
3. **Galam Escape**: At the end of a blocked path during the escape with Slade.
4. **Granseal Earthquake**: In a crack near the school after the earthquake (missable!).
5. **Ribble**: Center of the sandbox in the village square.
6. **Hill Path**: On a mountain ledge after crossing the bridge towards the Hobgoblin cave.
7. **Hassan #1**: In a chest behind a row of houses.
8. **Hassan #2**: Inside a wooden barrel on the docks.
9. **Fairy Woods**: In a chest near the bonus battle entrance (northeast corner).
10. **Secret Path**: In the dark cave revealed by Elric on the way to Creed's.
11. **Creed's Mansion**: On a mountain ledge in the northeast corner outside the mansion area.
12. **Pacalon Castle**: Down a hidden set of stairs in the northwest corner of the castle.
13. **Tristan Path**: On a mountain peak on the way from Pacalon/Moun to Tristan.
14. **Mitula Shrine**: In a chest on the east side inside the shrine.
15. **Sheela's Dojo**: In a chest in the bushes to the right of the training hall.

Crafting Advice

Crafting costs 5,000 gold per item. The weapon produced is random. 
Save your game before ordering, and if you don't get the desired weapon, reload and try again.
The probability of getting the "best" weapon in a category is typically around 4%.

Mithril Weapons Overview

- **Swords**: Critical Sword (+32), Battle Sword (+35), Counter Sword (+39), Levanter (+42). 
- **Katana (Slade)**: Katana (+34), Ninja Katana (+39), Gisarme (+42 - Chance of instant kill).
- **Axes**: Heat Axe (+32), Atlas Axe (+35), Ground Axe (+39, MOV +1), Rune Axe (+42).
- **Lances**: Valkyrie (+33), Halberd (+37), Holy Lance (+39), Mist Javelin (+42).
- **Bows**: Buster Shot (+37), Hyper Cannon (+40), Grand Cannon (+43).
- **Staffs**: Mage Staff (+27), Great Rod (+28), Supply Staff (+32), Freeze Staff (+37), Mystery Staff (+39).
- **Gloves**: Misty Knuckles (+48), Giant Knuckles (+55).
